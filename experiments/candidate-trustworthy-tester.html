What early testers help improve

Real-world sleep detection

Different mattresses, phones, rooms, and routines help reveal where the smart nap engine needs tuning.

Clear onboarding

Your privacy and setup feedback helps make the free version easier to trust and understand.

Better alarms

Try true-duration naps, hard deadlines, regular alarms, and the dark interface near rest.

Better insights

Use tags and ratings to see whether the history view explains your own rest clearly.

A focused alternative

Not a sleep laboratory

Naptime is intentionally focused on smart nap timing, with night tracking as context.

Not an audio sleep app

It does not try to soundtrack your rest. It times the alarm after sleep begins.

Clear data donation for the free test.

After you consent, anonymous session and usage data may be uploaded when you start a nap or sleep session. Installing or opening the app does not donate session data. No account, location, microphone audio, contacts, advertising ID, or raw continuous accelerometer stream is collected.
