How sleep-start timing works

1

Choose the sleep duration

Pick the nap you want: 10, 20, or 30 minutes of actual sleep.

2

Place your phone

Put your Android phone face-up on the mattress near you.

3

Let Naptime wait

The app watches for sleep-like stillness and starts the countdown after sleep is detected.

4

Wake by your cutoff

If sleep is not detected in time, the alarm still goes off by your latest wake-up time.
