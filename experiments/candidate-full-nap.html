Why it matters

Timers start too soon

Problem

Your nap should not start while you are awake

A normal timer starts when you tap Start. If it takes 12 minutes to drift off, your 20-minute nap just became 8 minutes of sleep.

Mechanism

Sleep-start timing

Place your Android phone on the mattress. Naptime uses motion from the accelerometer to estimate when you have settled into sleep, then begins the timer.

Safety

Your cutoff still matters

Have a meeting, class, pickup, or shift? Set a latest wake-up time. If sleep is not detected in time, the alarm still fires by your deadline.

Learning

Find your real nap pattern

Rate how you feel, tag what might have affected the session, and compare what works for you: caffeine, noisy rooms, stress, or different nap lengths.

Free means clear data donation.
