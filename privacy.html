Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 5, 2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Naptime ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

In the App

We believe your sleep data belongs to you. Creating an account is not required to use Naptime.

  • Usage Data: We collect data related to your naps, such as nap duration, start/end times, and motion sensor data used for sleep detection.
  • Storage: All of this data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this personal data to any external servers.

On the Website

  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics to understand website traffic usage. This collects anonymous data such as page views, device type, and referral sources. It does not collect personally identifiable information (PII).

2. How We Use Information

Because the app data stays on your device, we do not "use" it in the traditional sense. The app uses this data locally to:

  • Function as a smart alarm clock.
  • Display your personal sleep history and analytics within the app.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your nap data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to export or share it yourself.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses Google Analytics, a service provided by Google, Inc. Google Analytics uses cookies to help us analyze how users use the site. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
